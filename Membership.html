Membership or Day Pass Options

Choose from our variety of membership options and day passes to suit your fitness needs. Each option offers exclusive benefits and access to our premium facilities.

Membership Options:

  • Basic Membership - $20/month: Access to gym facilities during regular hours.
  • Premium Membership - $35/month: Access to gym facilities, plus unlimited group fitness classes.
  • Platinum Membership - $50/month: Access to gym facilities, unlimited group fitness classes, and personal training sessions.

Day Pass Options:

  • Single Day Pass - $5/day: One-day access to gym facilities during regular hours.
  • Weekend Pass - $15: Access to gym facilities on Saturday and Sunday.
  • Family Day Pass - $20: One-day access for a family of four (2 adults, 2 children).
